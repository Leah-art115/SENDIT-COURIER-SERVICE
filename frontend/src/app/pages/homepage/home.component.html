About Us

SendIT is a smart courier service platform that connects customers with local delivery personnel for efficient parcel transportation. We offer multiple transport modes with real-time tracking and automated status management.

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable delivery services by matching packages with the most suitable transport mode based on distance, weight, and delivery requirements.

We leverage smart algorithms and real-time tracking to ensure your packages reach their destination safely and on time, with full transparency throughout the delivery process.

Our Transport Modes

Bicycle Delivery

Eco-friendly delivery for small packages within 5km. Perfect for documents, small electronics, and lightweight items up to 2kg.

Roller Skates

Ultra-fast delivery for urgent small packages within 2km. Ideal for documents and emergency deliveries in busy city areas.

Motorbike

Fast and flexible delivery for packages up to 10kg. Great balance of speed and capacity for medium-distance deliveries.

Small Car

Weather-protected delivery for packages up to 25kg. Ideal for longer distances and valuable items requiring extra security.

Large Car/SUV

Spacious delivery for bulky packages up to 50kg. Perfect for electronics, gifts, and items requiring extra care during transport.

Smart Matching

Intelligent system automatically suggests the best transport mode based on package weight, distance, and delivery urgency.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my delivery?

Enter your order ID in the tracking section to monitor your delivery in real-time. You'll see your driver's location and estimated arrival time.

How are delivery prices calculated?

Pricing depends on transport mode, distance, package weight, and delivery urgency. Our smart system shows you all available options with transparent pricing.

What transport mode should I choose?

Our smart system automatically recommends the best transport mode based on your package weight, delivery distance, and urgency requirements.

How can I contact my driver?

Once your order is assigned, you can contact your driver directly through the app for pickup instructions or delivery updates.

What is the delivery time for local deliveries?

Delivery times vary by transport mode and distance. Roller skates: 10-15 mins, Bicycle: 15-30 mins, Motorbike: 20-40 mins, Car: 30-60 mins depending on traffic.

Can I schedule a delivery for later?

Yes, you can schedule deliveries for specific times or dates. Choose your preferred pickup time and delivery window when placing your order.
